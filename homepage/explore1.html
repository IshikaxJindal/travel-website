Goa

November to February

Discover Goa: India's Beach Paradise Goa, a tropical haven on India's western coast, is famed for its golden beaches, vibrant nightlife, and Portuguese heritage. Perfect for relaxation and adventure alike, it offers an eclectic mix of culture, food, and festivities. Top Places to Visit:

  • Calangute & Baga Beaches: Popular for sunbathing, water sports, and nightlife.
  • Old Goa: Explore historic churches like Basilica of Bom Jesus and Se Cathedral.
  • Dudhsagar Falls: A majestic waterfall amidst lush greenery.
  • Chapora Fort: Offers panoramic views, made famous by Bollywood.
  • Anjuna Market: A vibrant spot for shopping unique souvenirs.
Goa's charm is in its ability to cater to every traveler, from tranquil retreats to lively celebrations.
